Wordle Script

A C program utilizing the potential solution list and regex to ruin the game for those who desire minimal false self confidence from cheating at wordle.

By entering the correct information gathered upon the first guess, the script will drastically limit the potential answers with each additional piece of information given by the game. Throughout testing there was never an instance where the game could not be solved in 5 guesses, with 4 being the most common result. With some good guessing 3 is possible, arising about 20% of the time.
